1 🏆 Objective of the Game

Become Lahore's supreme Urban Developer by accumulating the greatest wealth through strategic property investment, rent collection, hotel construction, and landmark development.

The last player standing after all others go bankrupt wins — or, if the group agrees to end early, the player with the highest net worth (cash + property values) is declared champion and earns a place in the Champions Lounge.

Net Worth = Cash on hand + sum of all owned property prices + building values
2 🗺️ The Board

The board is a loop of 40 spaces set across iconic Lahore locations. Players move clockwise around the board on every turn.

Properties — buyable real estate in colour groups
Orange Line Stations — transit hubs; rent scales with ownership
Thokar Ada / Go — collect Rs. 200 salary each pass
Adda Jail — visiting or serving time
Chance — draw a Chance card; follow its instruction
Community — draw a Community card
Tax — pay Income Tax or Luxury Tax to the Exchequer
Suthra Punjab — collect all accumulated Exchequer funds
Suthra Punjab Exchequer: All fines, taxes, and penalties are pooled here. The first player to land on the Suthra Punjab space collects the entire pot.
3 🎮 Setup & Starting

Each player selects a token (🐎 Tonga, 🛺 Rickshaw, 🏍️ Qingchi, 🚌 Bedford, 🚐 Ford) and receives a starting balance of Rs. 1,500.

Starting Cash:
2× Rs. 500  |  2× Rs. 100  |  2× Rs. 50  |  6× Rs. 20  |  5× Rs. 10  |  5× Rs. 5  |  5× Rs. 1

All players begin at Thokar Ada (Go). The host rolls first; turns proceed clockwise. In online mode, the room host acts as the game master whose browser drives all logic.

In online multiplayer, only the host can start the game. All players must join using the room code before the host clicks Start.
4 🎲 Taking Your Turn

On your turn, click ROLL DICE. Two dice are rolled and your token advances the combined number of spaces.

🎲 Doubles

Roll two matching numbers and take an extra turn immediately. Roll doubles three times in a row and you are sent straight to Adda Jail.

💰 Passing Go

Every time your token passes or lands on Thokar Ada, collect Rs. 200 salary from the bank automatically.

After moving, any action required by the space (buy, pay rent, draw card) is resolved before the next player's turn begins.

5 🏘️ Buying Properties

When you land on an unowned property, you may Buy Asset at the listed price, or send it to Auction. If you choose to buy, the property card is immediately assigned to you.

Deed History: Every property keeps a full ownership record — who bought it, at what price, and in which round. View it by clicking the property tile on the board.

You cannot buy a property that is already owned by another player unless you negotiate a Trade.

7 🔨 Auctions

Any unowned property can be put to auction — either by declining to buy it, or through certain Chance/Community cards. Auctions are open to all active players.

🏛️ Urban Auction (30 sec)

Minimum bid = listed property price. Each player may submit one bid from their own screen. Timer counts down; highest valid bid wins.

🏛️ Govt Auction (60 sec)

If no bids are placed in the Urban Auction, a Govt Auction begins. Minimum bid = 50% of the listed price. If still no bids, the property remains unowned.
Online mode: All players see the live high bid and bidder update in real time on their screens. Each player can only bid for themselves — your bid controls are active only on your device.
You cannot bid more cash than you currently hold. Overbidding is automatically rejected.
8 🏗️ Building Hotels & Landmarks

Once you own a complete colour set (monopoly), you can develop your properties. Click a property tile on the board to open the Manage Asset panel.

🏨 Hotel

First upgrade on any property in your colour set. Significantly increases rent. Cost varies by property tier.

🏙️ Landmark

Second and final upgrade — only available after a Hotel. Triples base rent. The most powerful development on the board.
Building Rule: You must build evenly across your colour set. You cannot build a Hotel on one property while another in the same set has no Hotel.
You can sell Hotels and Landmarks back to the bank at half price if you need emergency cash.

10 🏦 Mortgaging

If you need emergency cash, you can mortgage owned properties. A mortgaged property earns no rent but gives you cash equal to half its listed price.

To mortgage: Open the Manage Asset panel by clicking the property tile → tap Mortgage. The property is greyed out on the board.

To unmortgage: Pay back the mortgage value plus 10% interest to lift the mortgage and resume collecting rent.
You must demolish all Hotels and Landmarks on a colour set before mortgaging any property in that set.
11 🤝 Trading

Players may trade properties and cash with each other at any time during their turn using the 🤝 Propose Trade button.

How to Trade

1. Click Propose Trade
2. Select the player to trade with
3. Choose properties and/or cash to offer and request
4. Click Send Offer

Receiving a Trade

The recipient sees an Incoming Trade screen with the full offer details. They can Accept ✅ or Decline ❌.
Both players' game states update instantly.
Online mode: The proposer sees a "Waiting for response" screen and can retract their offer at any time using the ↩️ Retract button. Only one trade executes per offer.
Mortgaged properties can be traded, but the new owner inherits the mortgage and must pay 10% interest to unmortgage them.

13 🚔 Adda Jail

You are sent to Adda Jail when you:

  • Land on the "Go to Jail" corner space
  • Draw a Chance or Community card that sends you to Jail
  • Roll doubles three times in the same turn

Escaping Jail

You must stay in Jail until one of these conditions is met:

• Roll doubles on your turn
• All other active players have passed Go at least once since you were jailed

While in Jail

You still collect rent from your properties normally while incarcerated. You roll the dice each turn trying for doubles — but you do not move until you escape.
Unlike classic Monopoly, there is no Rs. 50 bail option. You must roll doubles or wait for all other players to pass Go.
14 💸 Debt & Bankruptcy

When you cannot afford a payment, the Debt Collector panel opens. You have several options before declaring bankruptcy:

Step 1 — Mortgage: Mortgage your properties one by one to raise cash.

Step 2 — Pay: Once you have enough, tap Pay Now to clear the debt.

Step 3 — Trade: Propose a trade to the creditor to settle the debt with properties.

Step 4 — Bankruptcy: If all options are exhausted, declare bankruptcy. All your cash and properties return to the bank.
Declaring bankruptcy eliminates you from the game. The creditor does not inherit your properties — they return to the bank for others to purchase.

Government Nationalisation (Online mode only)

If a player disconnects and does not rejoin within 5 minutes, the government seizes their assets: all cash is transferred to the Exchequer and all properties return to the bank.

16 🏛️ Champions Lounge

Every game winner is recorded in the Champions Lounge — a monthly server-side leaderboard tracking the city's top property tycoons.

Monthly Reset: The leaderboard resets each month, giving every player a fresh chance at the crown.

Tycoon of the Month: The player with the most wins in the current month earns the 👑 Tycoon of the Month title, displayed at the top of the Champions Lounge.

Access the Champions Lounge at any time using the 🏛️ button in the sidebar leaderboard section.
